About the Film

The film follows Lebanon Hanover on tour in the fall of 2024, getting close to members William Maybelline and Larissa Iceglass. It explores the symbiotic relationship between the band and its fans and features performances from Bristol, London, Paris, and Berlin.

Duration: 48 minutes | 4K

About the Band

Lebanon Hanover is a Swiss–British post-punk duo formed in 2010 in England. The band consists of vocalist and guitarist Larissa Iceglass and vocalist and bassist William Maybelline. The trailblazing duo has proven to be one of the most influential post-punk groups of our time, a driving force behind the current scene revival with their uniquely somber and minimalistic, yet luringly infectious sound.

About the Director

Isolde Woudstra is a Dutch photographer known for her intimate, atmospheric portraits and documentary-style photography. She captures authentic, unguarded moments with an emphasis on the unspoken emotions and nuances of human experience. Woudstra has been working for clients such as Le Monde, KesselsKramer, Stern, NME Networks, and de Volkskrant, and her work has been exhibited at Foam, Unseen, and photomonth Krakow.
